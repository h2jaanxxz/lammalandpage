About Lamma

Our mission is to strengthen family bonds in Palestinian society through meaningful technology use.

Our Mission & Vision

At Lamma, we believe that technology should bring families closer, not drive them apart. Our mission is to transform screen time into quality family time through culturally relevant activities that strengthen bonds within Palestinian households.

Our vision is a society where technology enhances family connections, preserves cultural values, and creates lasting memories across generations.

Family First

We design every feature with the goal of strengthening family relationships and creating meaningful interactions.

Cultural Preservation

We integrate Palestinian traditions, stories, and values into our activities to connect younger generations with their heritage.

Safe & Trusted

We prioritize creating a secure digital environment where families can share experiences without privacy concerns.

The Challenge We're Addressing

Our Solution: Meaningful Connection Through Technology

Rather than fighting against technology, Lamma transforms it into a tool for connection. We've created a platform that encourages families to engage with each other through culturally relevant activities, games, and challenges.

By providing structured digital experiences that require family participation, we're helping Palestinian families reclaim technology as a means of strengthening relationships rather than weakening them.
