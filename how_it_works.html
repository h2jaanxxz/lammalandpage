How Lamma Works & Guide

Discover our innovative approach to family bonding through interactive activities and challenges. Although the Lamma app is still in development, this guide outlines our vision for connecting families in a secure and engaging way.

Step 1: Sign Up & Create Your Family

Register your account and set up your family profile. Begin by inviting all family members to join the platform.

Step 2: Customize Your Profiles

Personalize each family member's profile with unique details, photos, and preferences for a tailored experience.

Step 3: Engage in Fun Challenges

Participate in interactive challenges and gamified tasks designed to promote teamwork and strengthen bonds.

Step 4: Explore the Family Tree

Build a digital family tree to visualize relationships, celebrate heritage, and preserve memories for future generations.

Step 5: Share & Cherish Memories

Upload photos, videos, and voice notes to the secure memory vault, keeping your family’s cherished moments safe and accessible.

Step 6: Daily Interactions

Enjoy daily prompts, polls, and surprise timers that spark conversation and encourage quality time together.

Your Guide to a Connected Family

While the Lamma app is still in development, our vision is to create an engaging and secure platform that transforms how families connect. Stay tuned for updates and join us on this exciting journey toward stronger family bonds.
